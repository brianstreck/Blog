[Ingredients]

2-Cups Flour

1-Tablespoon Baking Soda

1-Tablespoon Sugar

1-Teaspoon Salt

1/3-Cup Shortening

1-Cup Milk


[Process]

Preheat oven to 425 Degrees (F)

Mix Dry ingredients

Add shortening, mix throughly

Slowly add milk until dough forms

Knead on flour 10-15 times


[Time]

Bake 13-15 minutes.

Total cook time, 30 minutes.
